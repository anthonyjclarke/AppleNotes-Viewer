Apple Notes Viewer
v3.0.0 — Local web viewer for Apple Notes exports

A zero-dependency local web app for browsing and searching your Apple Notes. Export your notes once, point the app at the folder, and get a fast three-column interface with full-text search, tag filtering, PDF previews, and light/dark mode — all running entirely on your Mac with no cloud, no accounts, and no internet required.

How to use

1. Install apple-notes-exporter (see credit below) and run an initial export with --format html.
2. Launch via Launch Notes.command or python3 server.py in Terminal.
3. On first run, click the gear icon (Settings) in the sidebar header to select your export folder.
4. Press Sync in the sidebar footer to pull in new and changed notes. Use Full Re-export when notes have moved to Recently Deleted or after major changes — it wipes the watermark and re-exports every note (takes longer).

Full setup and operational details are in README.md in the project folder.
