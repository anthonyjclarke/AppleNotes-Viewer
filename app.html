Apple Notes Viewer
v2.2 — Local web viewer for Apple Notes exports

A zero-dependency local web app for browsing and searching your Apple Notes. Export your notes once with apple-notes-exporter, point the app at the folder, and get a fast three-column interface with full-text search, tag filtering, PDF previews, and light/dark mode — all running entirely on your Mac with no cloud, no accounts, and no internet required.

How to use

1. Install apple-notes-exporter and run an initial export — pay careful attention to the export format (--format html) and output path options.
2. Launch via Launch Notes.command or python3 server.py in Terminal.
3. On first run, use Settings (⚙️) to select your export folder.
4. Press ↻ Sync anytime to pull in new and changed notes.

Full setup and operational details are in README.md in the project folder.
