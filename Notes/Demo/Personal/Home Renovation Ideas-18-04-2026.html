Kitchen and living room refresh — #ideas



Kitchen
Replace benchtop with Caesarstone (Calacatta Nuvo colourway)
New splashback — large format white subway tile
Cabinet handles — brushed brass, Ikea BAGGANÄS
Under-cabinet LED strip lighting

Living room
Repaint walls — Dulux Lexicon Quarter (existing trim staying white)
New sofa — look at Jardan or King Living
Pendant light over dining table — rattan or linen shade

Budget estimate
Kitchen: ~$4 500 · Living room: ~$3 200 · Contingency: $1 000
Total: ~$8 700

Get three quotes before committing. Talk to strata about benchtop work.
