New Employee Onboarding Checklist


Standard checklist for new starters — #work

Day 1 — IT setup
  • Laptop issued and configured
  • Email account active
  • Slack, Jira, Figma, Notion access granted
  • Building access card issued
  • Emergency contact form submitted to HR

Week 1 — People and context
  • Meet direct manager (30 min 1:1)
  • Meet team members individually (15 min each)
  • Read company handbook — note questions for manager
  • Shadow one customer support call
  • Review last three quarterly business reviews

Month 1 — First contribution
  • Identify one quick win to deliver
  • Document one process that is unclear or undocumented
  • Complete mandatory compliance training

Updated March 2023. Review annually or when onboarding process changes.
