Route 66 Road Trip


Chicago to Santa Monica — May 2024 — #travel
3 980 km across 8 states in 14 days

Daily breakdown
Day 1–2: Chicago — deep dish pizza, blues on the South Side
Day 3: Springfield IL — Lincoln sites, Cozy Dog Drive In
Day 4: St Louis MO — Gateway Arch, Ted Drewes frozen custard
Day 5: Oklahoma City — bombing memorial, cowboy culture
Day 6–7: Amarillo TX — Cadillac Ranch, Big Texan Steak Ranch
Day 8: Santa Fe NM — detour but worth every minute
Day 9–10: Albuquerque NM — Old Town, balloon festival timing (lucky)
Day 11: Flagstaff AZ — Grand Canyon day trip (absolutely do this)
Day 12: Kingman AZ — most authentic Route 66 stretch
Day 13: Mojave Desert, Barstow
Day 14: Los Angeles, Santa Monica Pier — dipped wheels in the Pacific

Hire car notes
Dodge Challenger — great on highway, terrible on fuel (~15 L/100 km)
One-way drop-off fee: $180 — worth it, don't do the return

Favourite moment: sitting on the hood watching the sunset in the Mojave.
Don't rush this one. Two weeks felt short.
