Universal Packing Checklist


Works for any trip 5 days+ — #travel

Documents
  • Passport (check expiry — 6 months validity required)
  • Travel insurance documents (digital + printed)
  • Hotel and flight confirmations
  • Emergency contacts card (separate from phone)
  • Visa if required

Clothing (carry-on rule: 3–4 tops, 2 bottoms, 1 dress/smart outfit)
Layers over bulk · Quick-dry fabrics · Comfortable walking shoes
One pair smart shoes · Light packable jacket · Swimwear if relevant

Tech
Universal power adapter · Phone + charger · Portable battery bank
Earphones · Camera (optional) · Local SIM or roaming plan sorted

Health and toiletries
Prescription medication (+ extra week's supply) · Basic first aid kit
Sunscreen · Lip balm · Hand sanitiser · Sleep mask + earplugs

Comfort on the plane
Compression socks · Neck pillow · Download films and podcasts offline

Pack the night before. Weigh bag before leaving. Leave room for souvenirs.
