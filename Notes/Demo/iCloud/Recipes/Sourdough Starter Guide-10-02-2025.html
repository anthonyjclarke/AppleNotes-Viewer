Sourdough Starter Guide


Starting and maintaining a healthy starter — #recipe

Day 1
50 g wholemeal flour + 50 g lukewarm water. Mix, leave loosely covered at room temp (21–24°C).

Days 2–4
Discard all but 50 g. Feed: 50 g flour + 50 g water. Repeat daily same time.
You'll see bubbles forming by day 3 — this is good.

Days 5–7
Starter should double in 4–6 hours after feeding and smell pleasantly sour.
Float test: drop a teaspoon in water. If it floats, it's ready to bake with.

Ongoing maintenance
Room temp: feed daily · Fridge: feed weekly (discard and feed before using)
Use 100 g of starter per loaf — remove before feeding

Troubleshooting
  • Hooch (grey liquid) on top — starter is hungry, pour off and feed more frequently
  • No bubbles by day 4 — try warmer spot, use filtered water (chlorine kills yeast)
  • Pink or orange streaks — contamination, start again
