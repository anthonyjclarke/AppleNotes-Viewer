Banana Bread Recipe


Perfect every time — #recipe



Ingredients (serves 8–10)
3 very ripe bananas · 80 g unsalted butter, melted · 150 g caster sugar
1 egg, beaten · 1 tsp vanilla extract · 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
pinch of salt · 190 g plain flour
Optional: 80 g walnuts or dark chocolate chips

Method
1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease a 23 × 13 cm loaf tin.
2. Mash bananas thoroughly in a large bowl — the riper the better.
3. Stir in melted butter.
4. Mix in sugar, egg, and vanilla.
5. Add bicarb and salt, mix well.
6. Fold in flour until just combined — do not overmix.
7. Fold in walnuts or chocolate chips if using.
8. Pour into tin. Bake 55–65 min until a skewer comes out clean.
9. Cool in tin 10 min, then turn out onto a rack.

Pro tip: freeze overripe bananas — thaw when ready to bake.
