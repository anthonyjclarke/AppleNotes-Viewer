Thai Green Curry


Quick weeknight version — #recipe

Ingredients (serves 4)
500 g chicken thigh, sliced · 400 ml coconut cream · 2 tbsp green curry paste
1 zucchini, sliced · 1 cup broccolini · 1 red capsicum, sliced
2 kaffir lime leaves · 1 tbsp fish sauce · 1 tsp palm sugar
Thai basil to serve · steamed jasmine rice

Method
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in wok over high heat.
2. Fry curry paste 60 seconds until fragrant.
3. Add chicken, cook 4–5 min until sealed.
4. Pour in coconut cream, add kaffir lime leaves.
5. Simmer 8 min on medium heat.
6. Add vegetables, cook 4 min — keep them slightly crisp.
7. Season with fish sauce and palm sugar. Taste and adjust.
8. Serve over rice, topped with Thai basil.

Use Mae Ploy or Maesri paste for best flavour. Avoid reduced-fat coconut cream.
