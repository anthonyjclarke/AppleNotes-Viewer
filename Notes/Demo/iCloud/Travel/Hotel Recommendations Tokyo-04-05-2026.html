Hotel Recommendations — Tokyo


Research notes — September 2026 stay — #travel

Shinjuku area (my preference for first visit)
  • Hyatt Regency Shinjuku — reliable, great views, ~$280/night
  • Hotel Gracery Shinjuku — Godzilla head on the building, touristy but fun, ~$160
  • Millennials Shinjuku — capsule-style, social vibe, ~$75 — good for solo

Shibuya area (good for first-timers)
Cerulean Tower Tokyu — stunning views, worth the splurge for one night
TRUNK (HOTEL) — boutique, excellent design, $200–$250

Budget pick: Asakusa
More traditional neighbourhood, cheaper hotels, easy subway access
Khaosan Tokyo Ninja (hostel, ~$35) or Remm Akihabara (~$110)

Tips
Book well in advance — Tokyo fills up fast especially Sept–Oct (peak season)
Aim for Shinjuku or Shibuya for first visit — central and walkable
Room sizes are small — pack light
